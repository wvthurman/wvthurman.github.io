About Vaughn

Vaughn is a freshman at the Cleveland Institute of Art. He is originally from Massachusetts but has lived in Ohio since 2012. In fact he lives only about 30 mins away from the CIA. Vaughn’s favorite medium to use in art is digital, although he also loves drawing and painting. Vaughn’s favorite things to do in his free time are play video games with his brother, draw on his Ipad, and eat Chipotle.
